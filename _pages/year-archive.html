Public Finance & Economic Development on the Texas Corridor

Texas has 254 counties, thousands of taxing entities, and an interstate that runs through the heart of it. Southbound 35 bridges research and practice on public finance and economic development — written for academics who study Texas communities and the state and local officials who govern them. With occasional detours into sports, politics, and religion.
